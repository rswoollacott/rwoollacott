westcoast fishing guide, architect-to-be, web developer

My life is based upon two geographical references: northern British Columbia, where I was born and raised; and Ottawa, Ontario, where I study Architecture & Urbanism at Carleton University.

Web development may seem a slight foray from architecture, but I tend to think of them as relatives in their respective two and three dimensions. User experience (UX), graphic communication, logic, quality of build and concept are significant aspects to both. The key to either is understanding a client's vision and responding with something that fits them as well as their favorite pair of jeans.
